Airbus Defence and Space - Internship
Cyber Security Specialist

I develop methods and tools to assess which threats can result in hazards. The ever increasing complexity of software and technology poses novel challenges for the aviation industry. More interconnected systems and autonomous decision making offer cyber criminals a myriad of new attack paths. Together with our international partners we develop tools which help designer of new flight systems to search for attack scenarios which their design is susceptible to. In particular, we focus on attacks which compromise the safety of the flight system. These tools can be used during operation as well to readjust risk assessments to new situations (say a sensor or engine breaks down). This research is part of the ALBATROS Project.
